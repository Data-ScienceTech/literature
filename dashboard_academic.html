Methodology & Tools

Data Collection & Quality Assurance

OpenAlex API

Source: OpenAlex.org - Open bibliometric database

Usage: Comprehensive metadata extraction for 23,266 papers from 11 AIS Senior Scholars' premier journals. We achieved 94.9% DOI coverage and 81.2% citation coverage.

Quality Filter: Focused on 6 journals with >75% abstract availability: Information Systems Research, MIS Quarterly, Journal of the Association for Information Systems, Information Systems Journal, Journal of Management Information Systems, Journal of Information Technology.

Data Quality Strategy

Approach: Quality over quantity - Instead of attempting enrichment from low-coverage sources (Crossref: 12.2% success, Semantic Scholar: 10% success), we focused on journals with inherently good metadata sharing practices.

Result: 100% abstract coverage enabling superior semantic analysis and clustering quality.

Semantic Analysis & Embedding

Sentence Transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2)

Source: Hugging Face Model Hub

Specifications: 384-dimensional embeddings, 22.7M parameters, trained on 1B+ sentence pairs

Selection Rationale: Optimal balance of performance, speed, and compatibility. Outperformed larger models (SPECTER2) due to PEFT compatibility issues while maintaining semantic quality.

Processing: Combined paper titles and abstracts into single documents, processed in batches of 64 papers, achieving 1.52 iterations/second on CPU.

Implementation Framework

Libraries: sentence-transformers, pandas, numpy, scikit-learn

Environment: Python 3.13.7, Windows 11, CPU-based processing for reproducibility

Community Detection & Clustering

Leiden Algorithm

Source: leidenalg Python package

Reference: Traag, V.A., Waltman, L. & van Eck, N.J. From Louvain to Leiden: guaranteeing well-connected communities. Sci Rep 9, 5233 (2019). DOI

Parameters: k-NN graph construction (k=15, cosine similarity), resolution parameter optimization (tested 0.5, 1.0, 1.5, 2.0)

Optimal Configuration: Resolution=1.5, achieving 28 clusters with modularity=0.669 and silhouette score=0.053

Graph Construction

Method: k-Nearest Neighbors graph with cosine similarity metric

Specifications: 6,556 nodes, 73,491 edges, ensuring connected components for community detection

Validation: Multiple resolution parameters tested to optimize granularity-coherence trade-off

Topic Extraction & Analysis

TF-IDF Vectorization

Implementation: scikit-learn TfidfVectorizer

Parameters: max_features=20, ngram_range=(1,2), min_df=2, stop words removal, text preprocessing

Purpose: Extract characteristic terms for each research stream to enable semantic interpretation of clusters

Temporal & Citation Analysis

Metrics: Stream evolution tracking, citation impact analysis, emerging topic identification

Validation: Cross-reference with domain expertise and established IS research classifications
