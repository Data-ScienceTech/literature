üìö Dataset Overview

This interactive dashboard presents a comprehensive analysis of 12,561 research papers published in the AIS Basket of Eight premier Information Systems journals from 1977 to 2026.

Using advanced hybrid clustering techniques combining topic modeling (BERTopic) and hierarchical clustering (Agglomerative), we identified 8 major research streams, 48 detailed subtopics, and 182 granular micro-topics that cover 8,110 papers (64.6% of the corpus).

Coverage Statistics:
  • üìä 8 AIS Basket Journals: EJIS, ISJ, ISR, JAIS, JIT, JMIS, JSIS, MISQ
  • üìÖ Time Span: 49 years (1977-2026)
  • üìë Classified Papers: 8,110 (64.6%)
  • üìë Unclassified Papers: 4,451 (35.4%)
  • üìö Citation Data: 980,661 citations (80.4% coverage)

üî¨ Methodology

This research employed a rigorous multi-stage analytical approach:

1Ô∏è‚É£ Data Collection

Comprehensive retrieval of all papers from the AIS Basket of Eight journals using OpenAlex API, including metadata, abstracts, and citation information.

2Ô∏è‚É£ Topic Modeling

BERTopic with MPNet embeddings for semantic representation of papers, capturing nuanced relationships in research topics.

3Ô∏è‚É£ Hierarchical Clustering

Three-level agglomerative clustering to organize topics into streams (L1), subtopics (L2), and micro-topics (L3).

4Ô∏è‚É£ Expert Validation

Cluster labels and themes reviewed and refined by domain experts in Information Systems research.

‚öôÔ∏è Technical Details

  • Embedding Model: all-mpnet-base-v2 (768-dimensional semantic vectors)
  • Topic Model: BERTopic with UMAP dimensionality reduction and HDBSCAN clustering
  • Hierarchical Clustering: Agglomerative clustering with Ward linkage
  • Evaluation Metrics: Silhouette scores, topic coherence, temporal consistency
  • Data Quality: 80.4% citation coverage, verified DOIs, standardized journal names
  • Dashboard Technology: Static HTML/JavaScript (no server required)

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Notes

  • Unclassified Papers: 4,451 papers (35.4%) were not assigned to clusters. These include very recent publications, highly specialized topics, and papers with limited metadata. They are included in the total count but not in stream analyses.
  • Citation Data: Citation counts are from OpenAlex and may differ slightly from Google Scholar or Web of Science. Coverage is 80.4% of papers.
  • Temporal Range: While the dataset spans 1977-2026, most papers are from 1990 onwards. The 2026 papers are early online publications.
  • Dynamic Research: Research streams are not static. Papers may bridge multiple streams, and classifications reflect dominant themes.
